# Moto Wektor — PRODUCT

**What:** Marketing website (static, multi-page) for **Moto Wektor Sp. z o.o.**, a manufacturer of
commercial van / light-truck body conversions ("zabudowy dostawcze", up to 3.5 t) in Bielsko-Biała, Poland.

**Register:** brand (design IS the product — a marketing site).

**Audience:** Polish fleet owners, tradespeople and companies that need a custom van body
(skrzynie/firanki, kontenery, izotermy/chłodnie, wywrotki, zabudowy warsztatowe, pojazdy specjalne).

**Goal:** present the offering, build trust, drive phone/quote contact.

**Tone:** serious, clean, industrial-professional. **Monochrome** (white / gray `#f3f3f4` / ink `#161617`
/ black panels); **color only in the logo**. Not Apple-like (left-aligned, solid header + dark utility
topbar, hairline borders, arrow links, dark footer).

**Current focus:** homepage hero is a **full-bleed background video** (client's drone + production footage),
with overlaid white text. Tuning: pacing, graphic harmony of the page with the footage, clean loop.

**Stack:** vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, self-contained, opens via `file://`, works offline. Fonts vendored:
**Archivo** (headings) + **Hanken Grotesk** (body). Motion: IntersectionObserver reveals + Lenis.

**Live preview:** `python -m http.server 5577` (optional) or just open `index.html`.
